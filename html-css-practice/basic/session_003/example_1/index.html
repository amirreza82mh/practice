Eggs are of my favarit foods. Here is recipe for for deliciously rich scramble eggs.

Ingredient

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in bow 1
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from edge the pan to the center every 20 seconds(as if you are maiking an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will continue the cook on the plate until served)
